Feature: Webpage Themes

Posted on 14.May 2015 | Tags pubbot.siteconfig

The below listed Themes are part of the “ Publishing Bot Basic Subscription”.

The Cloudburo Publishing Bot HTML generator is based on Bootstrap the most popular Web framework for responsive, mobile first Web pages and supports various themes. The themes itselfs are provided by bootswatch.com and are free for usage.

Via the Memberzone Dashboard (Setup Tab) you can configure the webpage theme.



You can choose one of the following themes for your blog, click on the theme title, to open a life sample showcase site.
