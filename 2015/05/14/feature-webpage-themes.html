    Feature: Webpage Themes

    feature memberzone

    Posted on May 14

    The Cloudburo Curation Platform is based on Bootstrap the most popular Web framework for responsive, mobile first Web pages and supports various themes. The themes itselfs are provided by bootswatch.com and are free for usage.

    Via the Memberzone Dashboard (Setup Tab) you can configure the webpage theme.


    You can choose one of the following themes for your blog, click on the theme title, to open a life sample showcase site.
