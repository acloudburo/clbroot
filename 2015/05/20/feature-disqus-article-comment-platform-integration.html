    Feature: Disqus Article Comment Platform Integration

    feature

    Posted on May 20

    Disqus.com is a no cost platform to build up a community by allowing people to comment your content site articles. You have to enable the Disqus comment feature in the Cloudburo Content Curation platform by providing your Disqus shortname. Each article will then be followed by the Disqus comment GUI component. You have to register with Disqus to benefit from this feature.


    As you can see on the screenshot above, the article is followed by the „Start Discussion“ screen of Disqus, which would manage the discussion sessions.
