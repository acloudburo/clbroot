Credit Card Payments Handling at Cloudburo

Posted on 24.Aug 2015 | Tags platform.admingui

Security is one of our biggest consideration, when it comes down to charge our client for our services via Credit or Debit Card.

Therefore Cloudburo choses Stripe as our Payment provider for handling any kind of credit card transaction. Stripe is a leading and trusted payment processor in the internet space.

Stripe has been audited by a PCI-certified auditor, and is certified to PCI Service Provider Level 1. This is the most stringent level of certification available.

We use Stripe.js on our website to collect the necessary credit card data in your browser. The entered data in the form is never sent to our server, instead they are sent directly to Stripe.

Why is this so important?

  • More secure because a breach of our servers won’t result in any stolen credit card data. They are only hold in the highly secure vaults of Stripe, which fully complies to the PCI standard.
  • Therefore we are automatically also PCI compliant because we don’t handle any sensitive credit card data on our servers.

Serving you with a premium service experience and ensuring highest protection of your sensitive data.
