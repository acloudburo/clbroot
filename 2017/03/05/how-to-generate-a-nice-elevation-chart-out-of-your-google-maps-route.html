How To Generate A Nice Elevation Chart Out Of Your Google Maps Route

Posted on 05.Mar 2017 | Tags pubbot.howto

The following article will show you how to generate a nice elevation chart out of one of your Google Maps Route which you have stored as a map in your Google Account (mymaps):

https://www.google.com/mymaps

In our example we want to generate a Elevation Chart out of our map “Küsnacht Walks”:


Having opened the Map in the Editor, select the KML Export feature.


Select the route which you want to export and download the KML file.


For the Elevation Chart use the GPS Visualizer Website: http://www.gpsvisualizer.com/elevation. Upload your file (1) and Draw the Elevation Profile (2).


Which results in a nice Elevation Profile, which you can integrate as Picture into your Blog.
