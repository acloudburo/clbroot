FAQ: How will I get notified by the Cloudburo Publishing Bot ?

Posted on 15.Jun 2016 | Tags pubbot.faq

The Cloudburo Publishing Bot will communicate via the following channels with you:

  • By default, the publishing bot will use the provided Email to get in contact with you
  • Additionally you can use the very cool and handy message app Slack. It’s our recommended app in case you want to start consolidate messages sent to you.

The Cloudburo Publishing Bot will send you a message in various circumstances, as for example

  • When a site got initially configured and is ready for use
  • When a change to site took place (e.g. a new article got published, or the site got regenerated due to configuration changes
