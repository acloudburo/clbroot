Don’t put unnecessary Carriage Returns at end of a Line

Posted on 29.May 2016 | Tags pubbot.quicktip

When you write in Evernote an article don’t insert unnecessary “Carriage Returns” (Line Feed) in order to go to a new line. You should keep writing and leave the new line switch to Evernote. The below formatting (taken into consideration the unnecessary carriage return) will result in a bad formatting on your site.
