    Showcase: Full Product Documentation Website managed by the Evernote Publishing Bot

    showcase

    Posted on May 19

    This is a special showcase due to the fact that this site which you are looking at is actually the showcase itself.

    I.e. the full documentation site of the publishing bot, enriched with images, videos and detailed content is edited in Evernote and published via the publishing bot.

    Actually, we are following the principle ‘eat your own dogfood’ within Cloudburo, i.e. we are using our own product for content publishing. This allows us to constantly fine-tune the offering and improve its usability and efficiency.

    Stroll around on our site and check out some pages to get a feeling how a full-fledged site could look like. Best is to start on the home page, which gives you a detailed overview.
