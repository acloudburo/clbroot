    How to sign up for the Cloudburo Bots Member Zone

    howto memberzone accountconfig

    Posted on May 15

    Creating a user account is straightforward via the link https://bots.cloudburo.net/sign-up

    For the creation of an account only your Email is required, as well as a Password.


    An Email will be sent to the provided Email Address and you will be asked to verify it. It’s mandatory that you validate your Email Address otherwise you cannot use any services.


    Open the Email and press the Link


    You will be redirected to Cloudburo Bots Platform Memberzone and a short message will be shown that the Email got verified. Pressing the Home button will redirect you to the Product Offering page.


    Clicking on the “GO TO PRODUCT OFFERING” button will bring you to our Product Offering. You can go on here:

    • How to acquire a trial product
    • How to buy a product
