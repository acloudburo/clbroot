    About



    This is the documentation site for the Publishing Bot from Cloudburo.net, which allows to publish content for your online community directly out of Evernote.

    Evernote is the place you write free from distraction, collect information, find what you need, and present your ideas.

    You will use the power of Evernote to manage and collect great sharing content, to compile it and leave the task to the Publishing Bot to deliver the content to your nicely, mobile-friendly designed blogging site.

    Concentrate on collecting and compiling content and leave the publishing to our Publishing Bot, which ensures a great user experience of your content on any kind of device.

    Cloudburo’s Publishing Bot will seamlessly integrate into Evernote’s One Workspace ecosystem to provide you with the best user experience.

    Being integrated with Evernote it offers you a superior front-end in order to manage all your curation and blogging content with an easy to use toolset and workflow.
